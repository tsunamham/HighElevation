Elevating Barber Shop Experiences

Style On Another Level

High Elevation Barber Shop is a locally owned Barbarshop in Alamosa, Colorado. We offer a classic barber shop experience while providing a broad range of classic and modern barber services. If you are looking for a great classic cut, a clean fade, or the best shave in town, we got you covered.
For the ladies, High Elevation is proud to be the home of some great stylists who are not only known for great cuts, but some of the best color jobs in the area. Everyone deserves a good hair cut and we are here to help.
